Community Bulletin

The city council has announced several exciting updates for our community. The new playground equipment at Riverside Park has been installed and is now open for families to enjoy. The playground features modern safety equipment and accessible play structures for children of all abilities.

Construction on the new community center is progressing ahead of schedule. The facility will include a gymnasium, meeting rooms, and a commercial kitchen for community events. Expected completion is set for fall 2025.

The annual charity drive exceeded expectations this year, raising over $50,000 for local families in need. Special thanks to all volunteers and donors who made this possible. The funds will support food assistance programs and educational scholarships.

Don't forget to register for the summer youth programs. Activities include sports camps, art classes, and STEM workshops. Registration deadline is June 30th, and spaces are filling up quickly.
